## Additional findings

Four tests moved from the main matrix to a new
`.test-matrix-rerun-on-failure` category: `btrfs_test`, `exfat_test`,
`ext234_test`, `hfsplus_test`. On first failure, the new
`.check-rerun-on-failure` template re-runs the test under `strace -vf
-tt` and captures the trace to `$TEST.strace.log`. The strace log is
captured by the `untracked: true` artifact setting. The `.check`
template was split into `.check-before-script`, `.check`, and
`.check-artifacts` for reuse across the three job types.
