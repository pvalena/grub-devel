## Additional findings

Affected targets: `mips_loongson`, `powerpc_ieee1275`,
`sparc64_ieee1275`, `mips_arc`, `mips_qemu_mips`. The change makes
these consistent with i386 targets that already use the portable macros.
`TARGET_IMG_BASE_LDOPT` resolves to `-Wl,-Ttext` for GNU ld and
`--image-base` for LLVM lld; `TARGET_IMG_BASE_LDOPT_ARG_SEP` provides
the matching separator (`,` vs `=`). Link addresses are unchanged.
